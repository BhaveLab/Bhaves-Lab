The Work

The Study Session
is structured observation.

Behavior is downstream. It is the result of something — not the source of it. Every habit, every reaction, every pattern you keep living originates in State. The internal condition you are operating from in any given moment.

The Study Session goes there. To the place where the pattern is actually being generated. Not to analyze it from a distance — but to observe it from the inside, while it is active.

In 90 minutes, you will locate your pattern, trace it through the sequence, and leave with a clear understanding of what is driving the experience you keep having. Not a concept. A direct knowing.

The 90 Minutes

What happens inside the session.

01

0 – 15 min

The Landing

We don't start with the problem. We start with State. Where are you right now — not emotionally, not intellectually — but energetically. This is established before anything else. HeartMath may be used here to create a baseline reading.

02

15 – 45 min

The Pattern Map

You bring a pattern — something you keep experiencing, can't seem to shift, or don't fully understand. We run it through the framework: State → Perception → Formation → Decision → Experience → Reality. We find exactly where in the sequence your pattern is being generated.

03

45 – 75 min

The Reflection

This is where the real work happens. Using structured questions, breath work, and real-time biofeedback, you observe the pattern from the inside — while it is active. Understanding from within is a different order of clarity than understanding from above.

04

75 – 90 min

The Integration

You leave with a clear, specific understanding of what is driving your pattern and what the next true move is. Not a list of tasks. A direction. What happens after is yours to decide.
