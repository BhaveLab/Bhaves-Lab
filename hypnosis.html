Inner Mining is not a performance. This is not therapy. This is not motivation. This is a deep observation process designed to slow the system down enough to see what has been running underneath the surface. Sessions typically run between 3–5 hours depending on the depth of the process and what opens during the field.

Before

Before the Session

Come with questions you want answers to. Patterns you've been noticing. Areas of life that feel repetitive, blocked, heavy, confusing, or unusually charged. An open mind and willingness to observe.

Nothing is off limits. This is a judgment-free space.

Session Flow

Four phases.
One field.

Phase 1

The Interview / Rapport Building

Approx. 1–2 Hours

The first part of the session is conversation. We slow down and go through your life, your patterns, your experiences, your relationships, your emotions, your perspectives, your loops. This is where the field starts opening.

Most people think they are coming in to "talk about a problem." What usually happens is the architecture underneath the pattern starts revealing itself.

There is no rush here. Some people cry. Some people laugh. Some people realize they've never actually said certain things out loud before.

This phase helps build trust between you, me, and your own system before we go deeper.

Phase 2

Inner Mining / Trance Work

Approx. 1–2 Hours

Once the system is ready, we move into trance. From there we begin the Inner Mining process. We move through different lifetimes, symbolic experiences, memories, impressions, or environments that your system brings forward.

We do not force the process. We observe what shows up and then ask: Why was this shown? What is connected to it? What pattern is still active? What understanding was missed? What is trying to resolve itself?

From there we extract the information. If physical ailments, emotional weight, energetic heaviness, or recurring patterns were mentioned beforehand, we may move directly into those areas to observe what is connected to them.

The goal is not fantasy. The goal is clarity. If the system recognizes the lesson, pattern, or attachment has completed its function, shifts can occur naturally.

Phase 3

Direct Communication

The Deeper Trance Layer

Once we reach the deeper layer, we begin direct communication with the subconscious. This is where your prepared questions become important.

Questions can relate to: relationships, purpose, patterns, fear, physical conditions, repeating cycles, emotional weight, direction, gifts, resistance, inner knowing.

This part of the process is usually where people receive the most clarity. Not because something outside of them speaks — but because the noise is finally quiet enough for deeper intelligence to come through cleanly.

Phase 4

Grounding / Return

Approx. 1 Hour

The final part of the session is grounding back into this reality. There is no rushing out of the process. We slowly reconnect: body, awareness, memory, environment, presence.

We go over what you remember, what stood out, what patterns were revealed, what insights came through, what may need further observation moving forward.

A session is not just about accessing information. It is about integrating it without destabilizing the system. Most people leave with a completely different relationship to themselves than when they walked in. Not because something was added to them. Because something underneath finally became visible.
