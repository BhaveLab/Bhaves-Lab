[ origin ]

It started in meditation. Not as a practice — as an observation. Sitting long enough to watch the mind move, Bhavé started noticing something precise: what was running internally was showing up directly in his external reality. Not metaphorically. Mechanically.

He didn't read that somewhere. He watched it happen. Repeatedly. The same patterns, the same formations, the same decisions — producing the same results. Until he started interrupting them at the source.

The framework didn't come from a curriculum. It came from years of sitting inside the exact conditions it maps. He built it by living it.

"I'm not here to solve your problem. I'm here to show you what's generating it — so you can operate from a different position entirely."

[ the work ]

Bhavé is a Source Practitioner. Not a consultant. Not a coach. He doesn't deliver strategy, solutions, or answers.

What he does is create the conditions where what's actually operating in you becomes directly visible to you. The pattern running your decisions. The formation happening before you choose. The mechanism generating your results — in business, in relationships, in anything.

Once you see it clearly, you move from there. The authority was always yours. It just wasn't visible yet.

[ who this is for ]

People who have already done the work elsewhere and know something is still running underneath it. People whose results keep reflecting something they haven't been able to locate yet.

Business owners, creatives, people in the middle of decisions that won't move — the surface doesn't determine whether this is right. The willingness to actually look does.

This isn't for everyone. It's for the person who is done managing the pattern and ready to see what's generating it.
