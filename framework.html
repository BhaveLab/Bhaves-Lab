The Pattern
Map

A structured method for locating where a pattern begins — before trying to change it.

What This Is

Not therapy.
Not coaching.
Cartography.

Most methods start by trying to change the pattern. This one starts by finding it. You can't move something you haven't located. The Pattern Map is a framework for tracing what you're carrying — a belief, a behavior, a recurring result — back to the moment it was formed.

Once it's located, it stops running automatically. That is the work. Everything else — the hypnosis, the biofeedback, the coherence training — builds on what this mapping reveals.

The Full Sequence

01

Name the pattern

Not the symptom — the pattern. "I keep picking the wrong jobs" is a symptom. The pattern underneath it is something older. We start by articulating what you actually notice repeating, in your language, without interpretation.

02

Trace the logic

Every pattern has a logic that made sense at some point. We map the original context — the age, the environment, the relationship — where this response was the right response. Understanding why it formed is not the same as excusing it. It's how you stop being surprised by it.

03

Locate the belief

Beneath every pattern is a premise — something the nervous system concluded and filed as fact. "Safety requires control." "Being seen leads to rejection." "I have to earn the right to rest." We identify the specific belief that's running the pattern, not a generic one.

04

Observe without collapsing

This is the part most methods skip. Before doing anything to the pattern, you learn to look at it without fusing with it. That distance — between you and the thing you're carrying — is where change becomes possible. The biofeedback tools make that distance visible in real time.

05

Introduce a different input

Once the origin is clear and the belief is named, the nervous system can receive something new. This is where hypnosis enters — not to overwrite the pattern, but to offer the part of you that formed it a different conclusion. Not forced. Not scripted. Specific to what the mapping revealed.

06

Measure the shift

Coherence changes. Brainwave states shift. The Bio-Well scan before and after a sequence is rarely identical. We track what's actually moving — not because you need proof, but because seeing the data closes the gap between believing something changed and knowing it did.
