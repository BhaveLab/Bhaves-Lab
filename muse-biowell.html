Two Instruments

Tool One

Muse EEG Headband

Consumer-grade EEG. Real brainwave data, in the room, during the session.

The Muse headband picks up electrical activity across four regions of the brain and translates it into readable data in real time. You see the actual brainwave state — not estimated, not inferred. The shift from beta (active thinking, defense, analysis) toward alpha and theta (receptive, calm, associative) is visible as it happens.

Used during meditation, breathwork, or before a hypnosis session, the Muse data shows whether your nervous system is actually entering the intended state — or whether it's performing calm while running the same loops underneath.

Beta (13–30 Hz)

Active thinking, stress, analysis, problem-solving. Where most people spend most of their time.

Alpha (8–13 Hz)

Relaxed awareness. The bridge state — calm but present. Where learning becomes possible.

Theta (4–8 Hz)

Deep relaxation, hypnagogic state, creative access. Where subconscious material surfaces.

Delta (0.5–4 Hz)

Deep sleep, restorative processing. Rarely visible in waking sessions without deep practice.
